About Eco Pigments

Eco Pigments is a natural food colorants manufacturing brand based in India. We specialize in producing chemical-free, plant-extracted food pigments that are safe for daily use. Our products are trusted by home bakers, sweet shops, bakeries, and catering services.

We follow hygienic one step processing, eco-friendly sourcing, and strict quality control to deliver premium quality of natural food colorants.

Usage Instructions

Shake well before use. Add 1–2 tsp at a time and mix thoroughly. Increase quantity gradually to achieve desired shade. Suitable for both hot and cold food items.
